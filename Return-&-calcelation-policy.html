Return & Cancellation Policy

Return Policy


  • 1. As a customer, you are entitled to a "no questions asked return and refund policy" wherein you can return the product at the time of delivery.
  • 2. You can return the product or products if you are not satisfied with the quality or freshness of the product.
  • 3. The returned product will be taken back by the delivery executive with no further questions asked.
  • 4. You will be provided a credit note for the value of the return products.
  • 5. This note will be credited to your account on the website. You can verify the amount credited on the Accounts tab of the website.
  • 6. You can use the credited amount for future transactions on the website.

Cancellation Policy


  • 1. When you place an order on the website, you will be allotted a specific time slot during which your delivery will be scheduled.
  • 2. As a customer, you can cancel your order anytime up to the cut-off time of the slot provided by TRSEPL.
  • 3. You can cancel your order by calling our customer care helpline.
  • 4. If you cancel your orders within the cut-off time slot, we will refund any payments already made by you for the order.
  • 5. If any fraudulent transaction is suspected by the customer, we are at our sole discretion to cancel such orders.
  • 6. If any transaction which defies the terms & conditions of using the website, we at our sole discretion could cancel such orders.
  • 7. We are at a sole discretion to maintain a negative list of all fraudulent transactions made on the website to prevent mishaps
  • 8. We are at a sole discretion to maintain a negative list of customers and would deny access to them or cancel any orders placed by them.
